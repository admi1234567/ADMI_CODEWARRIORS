ABOUT ADMI

What is ADMI

The Association of Computer Science Departments at Minority Institutions (ADMI) was established as a national organization dedicated to exploring and providing remedies to the educational issues in computer/information science and computer engineering that confront minority institutions of higher education.

What are the some benefits of joining ADMI?

Funding for research and student scholarships - Opportunities for collaboration and exchange of ideas - Increased visibility for your department and institution.

What are some ADMI's activities?

An annual symposium on computing - A diversity conference - A research grant program.
